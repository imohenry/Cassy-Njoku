SIMPLE EVERYDAY STYLING TIPS: A Girl’s Guide To Styling.

Cassy October 4, 2019

So, Let’s talk Fashion! Well, because basically the world is all about fashion now. It’s fashion and styling and I’m loving it, I hope you are too. It can get hard trying to catch up with the latest trends and chic styles but here I am to help with the knowledge you need for your ‘basic’ everyday styling. So here are 6 tips.

Tip Number 1: GET THE RIGHT FIT

Whether it’s skirts, whether it’s gowns or trousers, you always want to get clothes that flatter your body, making it look nice. I believe that when you get the right clothes with perfect fitting, everything just feels right. You get into the right mood and your confidence shoots up. So always get the right fit.

Tip Number 2: ROLL YOUR SLEEVES

Another styling tip you should live by and most fashionistas swear by is to ROLL YOUR SLEEVES. Especially for petite girls, when you roll the sleeves of your shirt it makes it look much better. Check out the image below 👇

Tip Number 3: TUCK IT IN

I know you’ve heard about this one several times but wherever you heard it from? They didn’t lie. Tucking your clothes in, whether shirts or tees, is a good styling technique to look put together. And most people don’t really know how to Tuck in properly or they find it difficult to, there are various videos on YouTube that’d teach you how to- even in different styles (Go check them out). So yes, tucking in completes your outfit effortlessly, it makes you look put together and good. It gives simplicity to your dressing.

Tip Number 4: CINCHING

Cinching your waist is another styling tip fashionistas swear by. The idea is to look and act put together and cinching is an essential key. Getting gowns with belts and using belts on trousers, skirts, blazers just draws attention to your curves. Cinching helps to make you look better, it also accentuates your hips. So cinch it!

Tip Number 5: INVEST IN BELTS AND BAGS

Now, don’t get me wrong. I’m not saying you should have millions of bags and belts enough to open a thrift store with lol but you should invest in very nice belts and bags because they flatter your dressing, compliment it and complete it too.

Tip Number 6: ACCESSORIZE

It cannot be overemphasized how important it is that you need to spice up your everyday look with accessories. Earrings, jewelries, sun glasses, watches. The point to this is not to overdo your dressing by accessorizing way too much, don’t go overboard with it, be careful with it. Simplicity is key.

These are just basic everyday styling tips I think we should all live by but then again remember to do you, be you, that’s more of the confidence you need because at the end of the day it’s your style, it’s how you speak to the world. So wear your confidence so ‘sexy’ and stay chic.

Quote of the day:Do something today that your future self will thank you for.
