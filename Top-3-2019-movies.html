Top 3 2019 Movies I Can't Wait To see

Cassy January 14, 2019

Hey guys, what’s been good? How was the weekend? Fun? Mine wasn’t really fun. But heyy, it’s Monday again, another day for the hustle. I hope you have a good day today and I thank you for stopping by to read, I appreciate ♥

So in 2018 we saw amazing block buster movies. From the start of the year 2018 to the end of it, the movies we’re amazing back-to-back. Producers, casts and directors literally had no chills, they just wanted to blow our minds away! Which they did

We saw movies like Blank Panther, oh my gawd! Black Panther? blew everyone away. I loved the depiction of the African culture and the fight for power, every scene in that movie was awesome. Also saw, Avengers:infinity wars, mission impossible, tomb raider, The Hate U Give, the Nun, Venom, Incredibles, Deadpool 2, Ant man and the wasp, Creed 2, Jurassic World, Chief Daddy, Black rose, God calling, Merry men and so other many amazing movies.

Now the one movie i cant get over so quickly? Is *drum rolls* KING OF BOYS by KEMI ADETIBA. Oh my, i saw this movie in November and i literally fell in love with it. From Sola Sobowale, to Adesua Etomi, to Toni tones, to Ill bliss to, so many names to mention in that movie, they all did an amazing job. Which if you’ve seen the movie, you’d agree with me.

It’s a new year and of course we are going to be blown away by the movies that are yet to be released. So without further ado, let’s get into it.

Captain Marvel

Opening on : March 8, 2019

Starring : Samuel Jackson, Brie Larson, Lee Pace.

If you’ve seen Wonder woman and Avengers and other marvel movies then you are sure to be marveled yet again by this one.

What Men Want

Opening on : February 8, 2019

Starring : Taraji P Henson, Wendi Mclendon, Max Greenfield.

Literally, most of us got to know Taraji P Henson from the popular EMPIRE, and since then we have to confess that we’ve fallen in love with her and her acting ( I personally have). So I really can’t wait to see this movie, though the release date is still a bit far but oh well😌.

LITTLE

Opening on : April 12, 2018

Starring : Marsai Martin, More Regina Hall, Issa rae.

Have you seen the trailer for this movie yet? You’re going to love it! Cos’ I already do. Marsai Martin is set to become the youngest producer in Hollywood films history, how great! I’m patiently waiting for this movies guys.

These are the top 3 I really really really want to see, but there are other movies coming out this year which are listed down below ⇩↓⇩

Us(march 15, 2019), Happy death day (Feb. 14, 2019), Where’d you go, Bernadette (march 22, 2019), Hellboy ( April 12, 2019), Under the Silver lake (April 19, 2019), Avengers endgame (April 26, 2019).
