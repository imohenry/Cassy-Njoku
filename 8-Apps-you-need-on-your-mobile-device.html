8 Apps You Need On Your Mobile Device.

Cassy February 7, 2019

Hey guys, what’s been good? I know I know, I been away for quite some time but believe me when I say, this was not a break I wanted to take. My phone got spoilt and at the same time my laptop got faulty too, sad yea? I know, but I got the laptop fixed and got a new phone….yay! Lol, so I’d be more consistent and on my A-game. Okay?

There are lots of apps on Play store and the Apple store and tbh most of them are irrelevant but either ways there are many useful apps on there too and most we haven’t even discovered yet. So the apps I’d list below are the apps I think that you need, to help with your day-to-day lives. Let’s begin!

1. Bible

Yes, the Bible app. I think this one is essential for everybody. Whether you are a Christian or a Muslim or of any other religion, there are apps for your religion there in both the play store and Apple store that you can download. Most times we don’t carry our Bible around so this app is essential, because you can easily open it at anytime and read the word of God and let it get into you and if you’re committed you can open this app everyday and read and learn the ways of the Lord and honestly when you start to make that a habit you’d start to get more closer to God and you’d see things in a different light. So please if you don’t have this app, go download it.

2. Wattpad

If you like to read stories, if you like to get yourselves engaged in interesting and thrilling stories then this is the app. This is an app where you find variety of stories, from thrillers to action to romance, to you name it! And they are so engaging and interesting and so many, you should try this app.

3. Eternal Sunshine

The name of this app is so different. It’s comforting, relaxing, it’s beautiful and it’s basically what it is. This is an inspirational app, yup! Most of us haven’t heard of this app before and when I first discovered it, I was very happy because I’m mostly on my phone and sometimes I need to hear motivational and relaxing talks that’d keep me going yunno, and then there came this app and I recommend the app to you, it’s available on both stores.

4. Duolingo

So you want to learn a different language? But sometimes it isn’t so easy learning off Google and it can be challenging too that after you’ve learnt you can’t practice with someone. Well I got you, this app is the nicest app there is, lol. You can learn ANY language on this app and guess what? They make it so fun, you have to come back each day to learn more. And there’s more, there are group chats for each language you have chosen to learn, where you meet other people and chat with them in that language thereby making you learn faster. Great right? I know. Go download it or if you don’t need it, recommend it to the person who does need it. It’s available on both stores.

5. Bubble Shooter

Some days yunno, you just want to relax with a game after a hectic day and all that, and the game you’re trying to relax with is just so challenging and annoying and trying to get you even more upset because you can’t pass a level you so badly want to pass?! Don’t worry, this game got you covered. Trust me when I say it’s relaxing. I feel like it’s the nicest game ever (lol, just my thought). My Dad introduced me to the game, lol he has lots of games on 3 different iPads, he’s such a gamer! And when he first told me about it, I didn’t feel like I would like it, but after I played two rounds? I was hooked, literally. I have other games on my phone, but I play this more. The pop of colour is beautiful. Plus the levels aren’t so challenging, it gives the idea that yes, it’s a new level you just entered? But you can pass this. It’s different from the games you’re used to. Plus, it’s not boring. So yea, download.

6. SoundCloud

SoundCloud is basically a musical platform that allows you upload, share and promote your music or audio. I feel like SoundCloud isn’t getting much recognition that it deserves because there are thousands of beautiful sounds and great contents there that people should actually listen to. So get acquainted with this app, if you will.

7. Merriam Webster

We need to learn different words everyday. IDC how busy you are but I feel like you should learn a different word everyday to increase your vocabulary. Yes, you need a dictionary app on your phone. And I like Merriam Webster more because of its display and how it gives vast meaning to words, makes it easier to be understood.

8. Pinterest

Yes, by now I’m guessing everyone knows about this app. If you don’t? Not to worry, I’m here to educate you. Pinterest is basically an awesome app. Lol. It’s a platform where you find anything and everything. When I first discovered Pinterest, I only used it to get fashion ideas not knowing there was more to the app. There truly is more to the app that’s why it’s an awesome app. So if you need to know more about this app, go download it my dear.

So yea guys those are the apps I think you should have on your mobile device. If you had them all already? High five to you and if you’re just knowing about them? Do download.
